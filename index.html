Project Overview

The Resource Management System is a centralized platform designed to streamline the reservation and monitoring of equipment and facilities within the College of Computing Studies. Whether it’s a projector, laptop, or lab space, the system allows users to view real-time availability and make reservations efficiently. It helps ensure that resources are well-utilized, accessible, and properly managed for academic use.
